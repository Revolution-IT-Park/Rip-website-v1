About Us

THE SOURCE OF REVOLUTION

Revolution IT Park is an Information technology company and interactive office set up in Dhaka, Bangladesh. We help brands to help them with their customers and build a relationship with their customers online and offline. As an Information technology company, we`ve been around since 2017 (which is a long time in internet years) but our experience as a service provider goes back a lot further. We are providing Website Design & Development, Web Application Design & Development, Mobile Application Design & Development, Software Development, UI/UX Design, API Development, Games development, Graphic Design. We are experts in creating measurable digital strategies all focused on converting visitors into customers.

Our Mission

We are constantly developing better ways of working. If we find a better way of working, we act upon it and proactively share it. With a human touch, our leaders and directors work daily to ensure that our employees are confident in their ability to carry out each client’s specific agenda. Consequently, it is our mission to not only build but to foster lasting and lifetime client relationships by delivering 100% customer satisfaction.
